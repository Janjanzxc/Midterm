About us

AISAT College - History

In 1998, the brink of the emergence of Technical-Vocational education in the Philippines, Mr. Ferdinand F Beltran spearheaded the establishment of Automated Systems and Technologies Incorporated in Pampanga as an answer to the need of people for cheaper education that will give enormous amount of skill-based instruction and experience instead of pure theories alone.

The institution thrive as they cater the clamor of families that are incapable of sending their children to high rising institution that offers great courses at a great price. In November 2006, Asian Institute of Science and Technology (AISAT) came into life as a trade name for ASTI. The change in name is in response to the growing population of the school and a marketing branding strategy of the institution to sound more formal and to give more meaning to it being an “educational institution” rather than just an institution itself.

In November 2006, Asian Institute of Science and Technology (AISAT) came into life as a trade name for ASTI. The change in name is in response to the growing population of the school and a marketing branding strategy of the institution to sound more formal and to give more meaning to it being an “educational institution” rather than just an institution itself.

The transition from ASTI to AISAT did not alter the founder’s compassionate vision of operating a “skills and values-oriented educational institution “committed to help in the nation’s development through education using international standards. It continues with its tradition of training students to acquire adequate knowledge and relevant skills meeting the needs of business and industry.

In keeping up with the founder’s commitment to help provide first class education with international standards, campuses were established on various dates in strategic places in Luzon. Currently, AISAT gives quality technical-vocational education to students in Dau Pampanga, Baliuag Bulacan, Fairview Quezon City and Dasmarinas Cavite. The school offers Associate degree in Computer Science, Computer Technology, Business Information Management and Hotel and Restaurant Management.

AISAT believes that true success is achieved through discipline and hard work. This is the reason that it gives just as much importance in instilling discipline and developing resourcefulness, perseverance and positive work attitudes in all students through AISAT’s values education seminars.

The school is now fostering a curriculum that will drastically change standard tech-voc teaching strategies and the curriculum is responsive to the needs of the students and the school prepares them for industry work rather than for unemployment. The great team of instructors coming from their own industry equipped with experience and skills in their line of expertise continue to harness the potential of the school’s growing population. AISAT continues to push forward to be the leader in Tech-Voc Education in Cavite

Mission

The Asian Institute of Science and Technology commits itself in the pursuit of leadership and academic excellence, in the fields of science and technology in an uncompromised quality. AISAT affirms its role in the formation of men and women to become highly competent and responsive individuals dedicated to service to the nation and the global community.

Vision

The Asian Institute of Science and Technology envisions itself as a globally competitive center of leadership and academic excellence in various programs with its students distinguished for their commitment to service, engaging in exemplary performance. The strength of the school will reflect the dedication of caring individuals who exhibit compassion and empathy for the diversity in others.
